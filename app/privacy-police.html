Personal data
Personal data is any information about an identified or identifiable natural person (data subject). An identifiable natural person is a natural person who can be directly or indirectly identified, in particular by reference to a specific identifier (name, number, network identifier) or to one or more specific elements of a physical, physiological, genetic, psychological, economic, cultural or social identity of a given individual.
Data subject
The data subject is a natural person to whom personal data relate and who can be identified by personal data or who is identifiable.
Special categories of personal data
This is a new designation for confidential personal data. We are talking about such personal data that, by themselves, may cause harm to the data subject in society, at work, at the university, or may lead to discrimination. Such personal data usually reflects racial or ethnic origin, political views, religious or philosophical beliefs, trade union membership, or genetic and biometric data is processed to uniquely identify an individual and data about the individual's health, sex life or sexual orientation.
Personal data administrator
The administrator is a natural or legal person who determines the purposes and means for processing personal data and is primarily responsible for the processing. Unless otherwise stated in these principles or terms for a particular service, VeganLoveLink is the data controller.
Personal data operator
The operator is a natural or legal person who, on the basis of the instructions of the administrator, processes personal data for the administrator.
Personal data processing
Processing is any operation or set of operations that are performed with personal data or arrays of personal data with or without the use of automatic procedures, such as collection, recording, systematization, structuring, storage, clarification or modification, search, viewing, use, access when transmission, distribution or any other provision of access, ordering or combination, limitation, deletion or destruction.
